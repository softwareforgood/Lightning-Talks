Sample Lightning Talk

Made Easy w/ Reveal.JS

This template is setup in order to encourage lightning talks.

Auto Advancement

Slides will automatically advance every 15 seconds, following Ignite's style of 20 slides at 15 seconds each.

Themes

Reveal.js comes with a few themes built in:
Default - Sky - Beige - Simple - Serif - Night
Moon - Solarized

* Theme demos are loaded after the presentation which leads to flicker. In production you should load your theme in the <head> using a <link>.
